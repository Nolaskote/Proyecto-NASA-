About Us

Who are We?

“We are the 'Elves of the Universe' team, a multidisciplinary group of Ecuadorians united by our passion for the cosmos. Our team is made up of two physicists, Alexander and Nolasco, a mechatronic engineer, Robinson, and a software technician, Adilem. We were motivated to participate in this competition with the goal of offering an innovative experience in the exploration of our Solar System. ¡We hope you enjoy our interactive model and live an amazing experience on your journey through space!”


What do We do?

"In 'The Elves of the Universe', we have developed an interactive web application that invites you to explore a virtual orrerie, where you can discover objects near the Earth in real time, either from the tranquility of your home or while enjoying a meal. With access to up-to-date information, we accompany you on this fascinating cosmic journey, offering an experience that is both educational and entertaining. Our mission is for you to have fun while you learn, exploring the universe like a true space traveler. From the history of the cosmos to the enigmatic sounds of space, this app is designed to spark your curiosity and give you the opportunity to teach and access specialized courses about the amazing world of the universe."
